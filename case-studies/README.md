## What Makes a Good Case Study (but not limited to)

- A real situation (anonymize company details if needed — the lessons matter, not the names)
- The constraints and context that shaped decisions
- What you tried, **including what didn't work**
- Measurable outcomes where possible
- Transferable lessons other teams can apply

## Ideas for Contributions (but not limited to)

- A migration story (framework, build tool, state library)
- A production incident caused by frontend code — and the fix
- A performance investigation from symptom to root cause
- A refactor that paid off (or didn't)
- How your team changed its testing or review process
